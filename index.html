What is Assemble?

Assemble brings people together on one platform to take action on issues that matter most to them. It is a web and mobile application where users can swipe through and select tasks based on their own interests and skills, drawn from existing local and national issue-driven organizations.

Why Assemble?

Users get a single aggregated platform for action; and organizations get access to new volunteers and data on the issues people care about and the actions they are most likely to take.
